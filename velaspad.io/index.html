VelasPad:  a Blockchain Like No Other

Built on the Solana codebase but augmented with a custom-built AI-powered Proof-of-Stake (AIDPOS) consensus system, Velas is designed to support the next-generation of decentralized applications.
As a virtual expanding autonomous system, Velas Chain is able to automatically react and adapt to changes in network load to ensure a consistent and optimized experience across all applications.
Able to sustain more than 50,000 transactions per second (tps), supporting smart contracts written in multiple languages, and providing a powerful opportunity for cross-chain interactions, Velas is uniquely positioned to host the mass market blockchain-powered applications.

Introducing VelasPad

VelasPad is designed with a singular purpose in mind — to help fund and support the development of the next generation of blockchain projects.
With the Velas blockchain moving to its mainnet, there's an incredibly rare opportunity to not only help guide the direction of the massively capable Velas Chain, but also help seed its ecosystem at the grassroots.
In true BlueZilla fashion, we will be democratizing access to some of the first projects building in the Velas Chain landscape — giving our users a second chance to be one of the early adopters and investors in what will likely soon be an expansive landscape.
Some of these projects have the potential to grow and become the Uniswaps, Aaves, and OpenSeas of the Velas ecosystem. Just imagine being one of the very first investors in the Ethereum, Solana, or Avalanche ecosystems.

HOW IT WORKS  HOW IT WORKS

Velaspad the first deflationary launchpad on Velas Chain

The main deflationary mechanisms of VelasPad include a 10% fee on all token sales (sell orders) and early unstaking fees of up to 25%.
Distribution of sell fees:
7.5% of the fees will be rewarded to stakers in VLX.
2.5% will be burned forever.
Early unstaking fees:
There will also be an option to unstake early. This will apply a 25% burn fee. However, if users wait 8 weeks to unstake, there will be no fee.
