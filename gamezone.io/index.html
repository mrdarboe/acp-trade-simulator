Unlocking the Blockchain Gaming Industry

Blockchain-based games are booming thanks to the advent of the wildly successful play-to-earn model and vastly improved accessibility in recent months.

But the true benefits and utility of these games are largely monopolized by whales, VCs, and hedge funds — who snap up tokens and game assets at rock bottom prices in early-stage deals, making it difficult for regular users to start playing.

GameZone Falcon punches the virtual walls that separate real gaming enthusiasts from popular new blockchain games to help users access the latest games early.

Deflationary Tokenomics

The long-term survival of the blockchain space and many of the projects building in it largely depends on the sustainability of their token distribution model.

Flooding the market with tokens leaves little room for speculators and opportunists, while releasing tokens too slowly limits the rewards available for early adopters and long-term believers.

At GameZone, we address both of these challenges simultaneously by carefully balancing rewards with a long-term token burn strategy. This will incentivize holders while providing a sustainable model of growth for the $GZONE token.
